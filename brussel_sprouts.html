Recipe: Parmasean Garlic Crusted Brussel Sprouts

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb brussel sprouts (serves 2)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp parmasean cheese
  • 1/4 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pepper

Preparation:

  1. Cut brussel sprouts into either halves or thirds, depending on sprout size.
  2. Mix olive oil, parmasean cheese, panko breadcrumbs, garlic cloves, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  3. Toss brussel sprouts in the mixture made above, coat thoroughly.
  4. Place on a non stick tray or tin foil with baking spray and put in oven on 350 degrees for 22 min.
  5. Remove from oven when crispy and let cool.
