Recipe: Bacon Wrapped Bleu Cheese Filet

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounce filet
  • 2 strips thick cut bacon
  • 3 tbsp bleu cheese crumbles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp salt/1 tsp pepper

Preparation:

  1. Thoroughly coat filet with 1 tbsp butter, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place second tbsp of butter in a frying pan until it heats up.
  3. Sear one side of filet for 3-3.5 minutes.
  4. Flip filet and sear other side for same amount of time.
  5. Pre cook bacon strips in a frying pan, but only for half of the time so it is still half raw.
  6. Carefully wrap filet in two strips of bacon, it should adhere easily if the bacon is not cooked fully.
  7. Place bleu cheese crumbles on top of filet.
  8. Place wrapped/topped/seared filet on a non stick oven tray and put in oven on 350 degrees for 8 min for medium rare, 9-11 min for medium, and if you do more than that you might as well just make a cheeseburger.
