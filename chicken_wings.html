Recipe: BBQ Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Chicken Wings
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp/1 tbsp pepper
  • Optional: 1 bag celery
  • Optional: 1/2 cup bleu cheese or ranch

Preparation:

  1. Cut chicken wings into flat pieces and drumsticks.
  2. Mix olive oil, onion powder, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Mix chicken wings with seasoning mix and coat wings throughly and evenly.
  4. Let cook in air fryer on 400 degrees for 25 minutes or oven on 400 degrees for 35 minutes. Make sure wings are not touching.
  5. Mix BBQ sauce and hot sauce in a seperate bowl.
  6. When wings are finished cooking, toss them in the bowl with sauce until coated.
  7. Cut celery stalks in 1/3 pieces and cut each piece in half down the middle.
  8. Prepare side cup with ranch or bleu cheese and serve.
